# Ghost Acres Atlas - Quick Reference Card

## 🎯 The Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"Urban civilization depends on invisible farmland thousands of miles away. Most cities have never mapped these dependencies. Ghost Acres shows you the ghost acres that feed your city, and predicts when those supply lines will break. We're making the invisible visible."

## 🌍 The Problem (60 seconds)
- NYC depends on 2.1M acres across 18+ countries
- Tokyo relies on 6 continents for food
- Most cities have <2 weeks of food storage
- When one region fails, 40+ cities are affected
- Nobody is prepared because nobody sees it coming

## 🔴 The Solution (60 seconds)
Ghost Acres does 3 things:
1. **Maps dependencies**: Shows which farmland feeds which cities
2. **Calculates vulnerability**: Scores cities on supply diversity, storage, local production
3. **Predicts cascades**: Shows how crises ripple across connected regions

## 📊 Key Statistics (Memorize These!)

### Vulnerability Champions (High Risk)
- **Dubai**: 92/100 - 1% local food, imports 80%+
- **Lagos**: 90/100 - <3 days storage, supply shocks are immediate
- **NYC**: 72/100 - 7 days storage, 3% local food

### Resilience Champions (Low Risk)
- **Sydney**: 48/100 - 15% local food, good diversity
- **São Paulo**: 45/100 - 18% local production
- **Berlin**: 42/100 - EU support, local farms

### The Ghost Acres
- **NYC**: 2.1M acres (size of Massachusetts)
- **LA**: 2.4M acres (most vulnerable US city)
- **Tokyo**: 4.5M acres (largest global dependence)
- **Shanghai**: 5.2M acres (feeding 25M people)

## 🚨 Failure Recovery

**If Globe Doesn't Load**
- Go to Compare page instead
- Show the city cards and vulnerability scores
- Describe the globe verbally: "Here's what you'd see on the 3D globe..."

**If Crisis Simulator Lags**
- Show a saved screenshot (have it ready)
- Walk through the numbers: "Day 30 shows -23% food access..."

**If You Lose Internet**
- Everything still works (data is hardcoded)
- Just explain: "In production, this updates real-time from USDA, FAO, satellite data"
