## 🎮 Demo Flow (Perfect for Judges)

### Opening (30 sec)
**"How many acres of farmland feed your city? Nobody knows. That's terrifying."**
- Start on home page
- Show stats: millions of ghost acres, dozens of cities, global coverage

### Exploration (1 min 30 sec)
1. Click "Explore the Map"
2. Click NYC on globe
3. Show the city panel:
   - 2.1M ghost acres
   - 72/100 vulnerability (HIGH RISK)
   - Top regions: Midwest, California, Texas
   - Only 3% local food production
   - 7 days storage capacity

### The Crisis (1 min)
1. Scroll down to "Crisis Scenarios" section
2. Point out the quick previews showing:
   - Midwest Drought: 18% food loss in 30 days
   - Drought + Fertilizer: 23% food loss in 30 days
3. Click "Simulate Crises" button
4. Go to crisis simulator
5. Select NYC, enable "California Drought" + "Fertilizer Shortage"
6. Show Day 30: 23% access lost, critical in 18 days
7. Show Day 60: 40% access lost
8. Explain: "This is cascade failure - one region's crisis affects 40+ cities worldwide"

### Comparison (30 sec)
1. Go to Compare page
2. Show vulnerability landscape:
   - Dubai (92): CRITICAL - 100% import dependent, 1% local food
   - NYC (72): HIGH RISK
   - Sydney (48): SAFE - 15% local, good diversity
3. Point out: "Geography is destiny for food security"

### The Pitch (1 min)
**"With this visibility, cities can build resilience through:"**
- Diversifying suppliers
- Increasing local production
- Improving storage capacity
- Negotiating long-term supply agreements
- Understanding their true vulnerabilities
