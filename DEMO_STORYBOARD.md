## Pro Tips for Live Demo

### 🎯 Pacing
- Home page: 30 seconds (hook)
- Globe exploration: 60 seconds (let them see the data)
- Crisis simulation: 60 seconds (dramatic moment)
- Comparison view: 30 seconds (show the landscape)
- About page: 30 seconds (explain the vision)
- **Total: 5 minutes (under 10 minute limit)**

### 🎪 Dramatic Moments
1. **Moment 1**: NYC lights up on globe - "2.1 million acres"
2. **Moment 2**: Show 7 days storage - "that's barely a week"
3. **Moment 3**: Activate crisis scenario - watch percentages drop
4. **Moment 4**: Show Day 90 - "-52% food access"
5. **Moment 5**: Compare Dubai vs Sydney - "geography is destiny"

### 🔊 Voice & Tone
- Start somber: "You have no idea"
- Build tension: Show the crisis scenario
- Shift hopeful: "With visibility, cities can build resilience"
- End powerful: "The question is: will YOUR city be ready?"

### 🖱️ Interaction Guide
- **Click** cities on globe (not too fast, let it load)
- **Scroll** through city panel to show all data
- **Click** simulate to activate crisis
- **Pause** at dramatic moments (day 90 critical)
- **Switch** between pages smoothly (pre-load tabs)

### 📱 Screen Setup
- Full screen, 1080p minimum
- Zoom at 100% (text clearly readable)
- Close extra browser tabs (cleaner look)
- Have about:blank tab for quick reset
- Pre-open globe, crisis, about pages in other tabs

## Backup Plan If Tech Fails

If the globe doesn't load:
- "Let me show you the data view" (switch to Compare page)
- Point to the city list and explain the vulnerabilities
- Describe what the 3D visualization shows without it

If crisis simulator lags:
- "Let me show you the pre-calculated impact" (show saved screenshot)
- Still explain the scenario and its implications
- Talk through the numbers

If Supabase is down:
- Not critical for this demo (auth not shown)
- All data is hardcoded, works offline
- No actual dependency on backend
